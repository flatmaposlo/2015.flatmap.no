LOCATION

flatMap(Oslo) 2015 will be held at DogA - the Norwegian Centre for Design and Architecture. This refurbished old transformer station is a meeting place for design, architecture and related subjects.

DogA - is situated in Hausmanns gate 16 beside the Akerselva river, not far from Grünerløkka in Oslo.

flatMap(Oslo) - A functional programming conference, with focus on Scala and the Java Virtual Machine.

On the 12th and 13th of May 2014, a group of enthusiasts organised the third flatMap(Oslo) conference. It was 2 amazing days. 120 attendees gathered for technical talks, workshops, discussions and craft beer. Feedback was great, and in 2015 we gather again for the 4th annual flatMap(Oslo). The participant goal for this year is the same. Small and intimate, but with high ambitions. Also, we can't scale past the capacity of our brewery!

flatMap(Oslo) is organised by a group of developers from Arktekk – a small independent consultancy company based in Oslo, Norway. Our financial goal for flatMap(Oslo) is to break even. Every penny we make will go into making the best possible conference. Together with our awesome sponsors we work hard to give you a conference tailored for enthusiasts of Scala and functional programming on the Java Virtual Machine.
